Sorting Visualizer

This is a simple sorting visualizer demonstrating the Bubble Sort and Quick Sort algorithms. You can generate a new array of bars with random heights, adjust the array size and animation speed, and then watch the algorithm sort the bars visually. The blue bars represent unsorted elements. During a swap, they will temporarily turn red and green, and during comparisons or partitioning in Quick Sort, they may turn yellow. Sorted elements will eventually turn green.
